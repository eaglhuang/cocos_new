# Bloodline Mirror Awakening Prompt v3

## 用途

- 英靈升星 / 二轉突破 / 血脈覺醒頁 `v3`
- 也可當 `血脈命鏡 Loading / 過場圖` 的精修母型
- 核心是 `左表象面 / 右血脈面 / 中央命鏡裂隙 / 中央定心者 / 下方命運故事帶`

## Gemini Prompt

```text
Please generate a high-quality game UI concept illustration for a Three Kingdoms hero awakening screen.

This is a special breakthrough, ascension, second evolution, spirit awakening, or bloodline mirror loading page, not a normal daily profile page.

Use Zhao Yun as the example hero.

Core concept:
One hero contains two coexisting destinies within the same soul.
The screen should feel like a mirror-world awakening ritual.

Layout:
- 16:9 horizontal composition
- upper area: two large mirrored Zhao Yun figures
- center: an ancient spiritual mirror-rift or destiny gate
- lower center: a small meditating human vessel, symbolizing the person who carries both identities within one body and must cultivate inner stillness to unite the soul
- bottom: a wide continuous 5-panel story strip reading from left to right

Upper left:
Show the public heroic Zhao Yun, solid, bright, human, noble, and clear.
This side represents his visible historical self.
Use lighter tones and a real-world battlefield or mortal-world atmosphere.

Upper right:
Show Zhao Yun again as a semi-transparent bloodline-spirit self.
He must still clearly be Zhao Yun, not just a generic ghost warrior.
The face, silhouette, hairstyle, and armor logic should all feel recognizably Zhao Yun.
This bloodline form should feel more ancient, fate-bound, inward, and spiritually sharpened, with personalized sigils, bloodline motifs, and a specific inner expression.
Do not make him look like a random blue fantasy soul.

Center:
Show a luminous mirror-rift, destiny gate, or ancient soul mirror connecting both sides.
This center should feel old, sacred, metaphysical, and symbolic, not sci-fi, not flashy fantasy portal effects.
The gate should feel more like an ancient fate mirror, relic threshold, or destiny fissure than a generic magical vortex.

Blending rule:
The left and right sides should not feel hard-separated like a comparison chart.
They should gradually blend toward the center with mist, light, subtle sigils, and emotional tonal fusion.
Each side should contain a small trace of the other, as if the human self already contains part of the bloodline self, and the bloodline self still contains part of the human self.

Lower center:
Place a small meditating human figure below the center gate.
This figure symbolizes the vessel that contains both selves.
It should be visually meaningful but not larger than the two main Zhao Yun figures.
Its glow color can indicate rarity tier:
- gold for legendary
- blue for rare
- purple for epic
This figure should feel calm, sacred, and inward, not religious and not overly regional.

Bottom story strip:
Create one long connected 5-panel illustrated story strip across the bottom.
It should read as one continuous life-and-bloodline story from left to right.
Panel rhythm:
1. mortal beginning
2. heroic rise
3. crisis or sacrifice
4. bloodline awakening or mirror-contact moment
5. spirit continuation, inherited destiny, or ancestral truth

This strip should feel like a fate-scroll or life-story carousel, but still remain readable.

Visual style:
Premium fantasy-strategy game awakening UI with elegant international adaptability.
The design must not rely too heavily on rigid Chinese historical symbols.
Do not use heavy calligraphy, antique overload, cyberpunk, sci-fi interface logic, horror corruption, monster mutation, or western fantasy dragon imagery.
Use elegant contrast, parchment and night-toned duality, refined sigils, subtle mystical framing, moonlight, muted gold, muted teal, and atmospheric fusion.

Overall feeling:
This should feel like Zhao Yun is not merely leveling up, but awakening the deeper inherited destiny hidden within him.
It should feel powerful, emotional, symbolic, premium, and suitable both for awakening presentation and loading-screen worldbuilding.
```

## 精修重點

1. 右側英靈趙雲要更像「趙雲本人」，不是 generic 靈魂武將。
2. 中央門要從一般 fantasy 發光門，收斂成「古老命鏡 / 命格裂隙」。
3. 下方第 4、5 格要更明確指向血脈真相與英靈延續。
